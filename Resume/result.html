What have I learned in my group exercises?

Practice and improve soft skills Advanced

I was originally a shy and timid person, but after a period of working in groups with people, my communication skills have improved. Because teamwork will contribute to confidence, making it easier for me to give appropriate opinions and perspectives to contribute to my organization.

Abundant professional knowledge and work experience Advanced

In addition to improving my skills, teamwork is also a very effective way for me to learn new knowledge. Each member of the organization owns their own treasure of knowledge. And in the process of listening to people present, speak, and give opinions, you will have more useful lessons for yourself. If I stand as a leader, I can also practice more management skills and work organization in the team.

Improve discipline Advanced

If I work individually, I can freely decide what to do first, and what to do later, even if I miss the deadline, working in groups will absolutely not appear in these cases. Everyone is assigned the right tasks, and I have to complete the assigned work on time so as not to affect the team's productivity.

Create a long-term relationshipt Advanced

The benefits of teamwork are not only evident in the short term, but also in the long run. Including the relationship of each member of the team. After a period of contact, learning, and working together, you are not only colleagues but also close friends and comrades.

Listening skill Advanced

Your opinion is not always right. In order for the group work session to be quality, I need to know how to listen to the opinions of people around me. Not only did my plan improve, but my listening skills also helped increase cohesion among team members.

Communication skill Advanced

Teamwork means working together. Therefore, it is not possible for you to just sit still and listen to people discuss. You must also participate in the conversation as a member, both listening and contributing.

Work arrangement skills Advanced

In the form of teamwork, each person will be responsible for a different area. Therefore, the skill of organizing and dividing work will be extremely necessary. To enhance this skill, learn the advantages and disadvantages of each member's way of working. Then, arrange and divide so that each person's work productivity reaches the highest.
